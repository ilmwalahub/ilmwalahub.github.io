Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 18, 2026

Welcome to ILMWALA HUB. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how ILMWALA HUB ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, stores, and protects your information when you use our website, student portal, teacher portal, or any of our online educational services.

By accessing or using any ILMWALA HUB service, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone / WhatsApp number
  • Date of birth (where required)
  • Parent or guardian details (where applicable)
  • Profile photograph (optional)
  • Student ID and account credentials

Academic Information

  • Enrolled courses
  • Attendance records
  • Assessment results
  • Assignment submissions
  • Teacher feedback
  • Learning progress

Payment Information

When applicable, we may collect transaction references, payment dates, and payment status. We do not store your debit or credit card information on our servers.

Technical Information

  • IP address
  • Browser type
  • Device information
  • Operating system
  • Pages visited
  • Session information
  • Cookies and similar technologies

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Create and manage your account
  • Enroll you in educational programs
  • Deliver live and recorded classes
  • Track academic progress
  • Communicate important announcements
  • Provide academic and technical support
  • Process registrations and payments
  • Improve our services
  • Prevent fraud and unauthorized access
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Student Portal Information

When using the ILMWALA HUB Student Portal, we may store:

  • Attendance
  • Course enrollments
  • Assignment submissions
  • Test and examination results
  • Download history of learning resources
  • Account activity
  • Notifications
  • Login history for security purposes

4. Teacher Portal Information

Teachers using our platform may have information stored including:

  • Name and contact details
  • Assigned courses
  • Class schedules
  • Student records relevant to their teaching
  • Uploaded educational resources
  • Attendance and grading records

5. Cookies

Our website and portals may use cookies to:

  • Keep you signed in
  • Remember your preferences
  • Improve website performance
  • Understand website usage
  • Enhance security

6. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

Information may only be shared when necessary with:

  • Payment service providers
  • Technology and hosting providers
  • Communication service providers
  • Government or legal authorities where required by law

7. Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect your personal information. However, no electronic transmission or storage system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

8. Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to provide our services, maintain academic records, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

9. Your Rights

You may have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request corrections
  • Request deletion of eligible information
  • Update your contact details
  • Withdraw consent where applicable
  • Request information about how your data is processed

10. Children's Privacy

Many of our students are under 18 years of age. Parents or legal guardians may provide information or consent where required.

11. Third-Party Services

Our platform may integrate with trusted third-party services, including but not limited to:

  • Zoom
  • Google Workspace
  • WhatsApp
  • Payment providers
  • Cloud hosting providers

12. External Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices or content.

13. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available on our website with the updated Effective Date.

14. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
